The Origin

A Journey Inspired by Rumi

Jalaluddin Rumi was one of the world's most influential poets and spiritual thinkers. His message continues to inspire millions of people across cultures and generations.

Love connects us. Wisdom transforms us.

Rumi Room is inspired by this vision — creating a place where people can explore deeper questions of life through music, poetry, creativity, and conversation.

The Philosophy of Rumi

Love Beyond Boundaries

Rumi taught that the human heart has the capacity to rise above differences and discover a deeper connection with others. His message speaks about:

Compassion Understanding Humility Inner awareness The search for meaning

Rumi Room welcomes people from all backgrounds who are interested in wisdom, creativity, and personal growth.

The Language of the Heart

Music as the Language of the Heart

Music has always been a bridge between people. A melody can express emotions that words cannot describe.

The sound of the Ney, the beauty of traditional melodies, and the silence between notes invite us to slow down, listen, and experience the deeper dimensions of life.

At Rumi Room, music is not only something we hear. It is something we feel.

The Voice of the Ney

The Ney is a simple reed instrument with a powerful story. For centuries, its sound has represented the human journey — the longing for meaning, connection, and inner peace.

Through the voice of the Ney, we explore the relationship between breath, sound, silence, and the human spirit.

"Dinle neyden, nasıl hikâyet eder
Ayrılıklardan şikâyet eder."

Listen to the reed, and how it tells its story — a story of longing and separation.
— Mevlâna, Mesnevî

Learn, Practice, Heal

Rumi Room Programs

Beyond our gatherings, Rumi Room offers ongoing paths for those who wish to go deeper — learning an instrument, joining a lineage of shared practice, or simply finding a quieter mind through sound.

Instruction

Ney Lessons

Guided instruction in breath, technique, and the meditative discipline of the reed flute — open to beginners and returning students alike.

Tradition

Meşk Gatherings

Traditional master-to-student circles where music is learned by listening, repetition, and shared presence — carrying forward a living oral tradition.

Wellbeing

Music Therapy

Sessions exploring how sound, rhythm, and silence can support emotional wellbeing, reflection, and inner balance, guided by trained facilitators.

Ambiance

Mystic Evenings

Candlelight, incense, and stillness — evenings shaped purely to hold a contemplative, mystical atmosphere for reflection and inner quiet.

About Rumi Room

A Cultural and Artistic Initiative

Rumi Room is a cultural and artistic initiative inspired by the wisdom of Jalaluddin Rumi.

Our purpose is to create a space where people can discover beauty, knowledge, and connection through music, poetry, and thoughtful dialogue.

We believe that every person carries an inner journey — a search for understanding, peace, and meaning.

Rumi Room invites everyone to participate in this journey.
